Fasd Printer v0.1

DCPU-16 Hardware Info:
    Name: Printer
    ID: 0x475e72c3, version: 0x0001
    Manufacturer: 0xFA5D0000 (Fasd)

Description:
    Fasd Printer can write data to stdout.

Interrupt behavior:
    When a HWI is received by the Printer, it reads the A register and does one
    of the following actions:

0x0000: write to stdout (words)
    Write C words form address B to stdout, if B+C>0x10000, A will be 0xFFFF and the process fails. It will only write C words or nothing. A will be 0x0001 when succeeded, 0x0000 when failed.
